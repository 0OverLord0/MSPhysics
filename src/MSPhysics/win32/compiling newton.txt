Compiling Newton

Read this post for details on compiling Newton:
http://newtondynamics.com/forum/viewtopic.php?f=9&t=8508#p55371


Compile Newton for MSPhysics with the following settings:

- Disable threads, see post for details. Threads in SU are unstable and affect
  performance.

- Open ../NewtonDynamics/coreLibrary_300/source/newton/NewtonClass.h, and scroll
  down to line 31:
    a. Set DG_MAX_TIMESTEP to 1/30.0
    b. Set DG_MIN_TIMESTEP to 1/1200.0
